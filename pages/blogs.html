AlgoByte Induction Ceremony
Officially kickstarting this journey with the first ever official open source community ALGOBYTE, our first event was the INDUCTION CEREMONY organized on the 17th of December 2022 in the evening at Nav Mandir Auditorium 2.

Seated on the dais, were our chief guests dr. CK Jha, HOD of the AIM and ACT department, Dr. Abhishek, CEO of the atal incubation center, Banasthali Vidyapith, and our faculty mentor Dr. Vaibhav Vyas, associate professor, Banasthali Vidyapith. Madhugandha, our co-founder started off the event by giving an introductory speech, briefing the audience about the club.

Then Dr. Abhishek addressed the audience with a simple and exemplary speech. He stressed " the importance of adapting to new technologies and innovations for engineers. " Madhugandha then apprised the audience about the Algobyte, our aim, and our upcoming projects. The event was then taken forward by a motivational speech by the mentor of the club Dr. Vaibhav Vyas, who gave students positive energy and lots of motivation.

Dr. Chandra Kumar Jha, HOD of the AIM and ACT department spoke about the equal importance of all branches. He also congratulated the team members and motivated them to hold hackathons and workshops.

After that, there was a QnA session held to make the program more interactive, the students who answered the questions were given gifts as a token of encouragement. Nandini Mallick, Our cofounder wrapped up the event by thanking the audience and the faculty members. We, the team of Algobyte showcase our heartfelt gratitude to all of you for making the Induction Ceremony so magical.

AlgoByte IoT Session
The Internet of Things (IoT) is a rapidly growing field that is changing the way we live and work. The concept of IoT is about connecting devices and machines to the internet, allowing them to communicate with each other and share data. In recent years, IoT has become increasingly popular, with more and more devices being connected to the internet every day. The session conducted on IoT by Algobyte in collaboration with IIT Madras was very informative and helpful.

The session provided an introduction to IoT and its applications. The instructor explained how IoT is transforming various industries, including healthcare, agriculture, manufacturing, and smart homes. It was interesting to learn how IoT devices can monitor and control various aspects of our daily lives, from home appliances to health and fitness devices.

The session also covered the basic components of an IoT system, including sensors, actuators, and microcontrollers. The instructor explained how these components work together to collect and transmit data, and how they can be used to develop IoT applications. This was helpful in understanding the technical aspects of IoT and the challenges that developers face when working on IoT projects.

And lastly the session provided hands-on experience with IoT devices and platforms. We were able to work with popular IoT devices like the Raspberry Pi and Arduino, and learn how to develop and deploy IoT applications using platforms like AWS IoT and Google Cloud IoT. This hands-on experience helped to reinforce the concepts we learned in the session and provided us with valuable practical experience.
